# Content Calendar

## Weekly Cadence
| Day | Channel | Content Type | Owner |
|-----|---------|-------------|-------|
| Monday | Blog | Technical deep-dive or tutorial | Content Creator |
| Tuesday | Twitter/X | Thread (3-5 tweets) | Social Media Manager |
| Wednesday | LinkedIn | Thought leadership post | CMO |
| Thursday | Twitter/X | Demo clip (30-60s) | Designer + Social Media Manager |
| Friday | Blog/Social | Build-in-public update | Content Creator |

## Monthly Campaigns
- Week 1: Feature spotlight
- Week 2: Technical deep-dive
- Week 3: Community/ecosystem content
- Week 4: Retrospective + next month preview

## Content Pipeline
1. **Ideation** → CMO adds to calendar
2. **Creation** → Content Creator / Designer produces draft
3. **Review** → Content Reviewer checks accuracy, voice, SEO
4. **Approval** → CMO approves, CEO for major campaigns
5. **Publish** → Social Media Manager schedules and posts
6. **Measure** → CMO reviews engagement metrics weekly

## Approval Gates
- Blog posts: Content Reviewer + CMO
- Twitter threads: Content Reviewer
- Launch campaigns: CEO + CMO + Content Reviewer
- Visual assets: Designer self-review + CMO
